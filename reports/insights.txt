CareMetrics Project - Insights Report

---

1. Key Findings

---

* Peak hospital activity occurs during mid-day hours (around 9 AM – 12 PM)
* Patient flow is highest during consultation and diagnostics stages
* Average waiting time is moderate but increases during peak hours

---

2. Bottleneck Analysis

---

* Diagnostics stage shows higher delays compared to other stages
* Consultation queues build up during peak hours
* Billing stage has consistent but smaller delays

---

3. Capacity Insights

---

* Event distribution shows uneven workload across stages
* Highest number of events occur during specific hours of the day
* Indicates need for better resource allocation during peak time

---

4. Recommendations

---

* Increase staffing during peak hours (morning to noon)
* Optimize diagnostic processes to reduce delays
* Improve queue management in consultation stage
* Introduce better scheduling to balance patient load

---

5. Conclusion

---

The analysis helps identify inefficiencies in hospital workflow.
By optimizing resource allocation and reducing bottlenecks,
overall patient experience and operational efficiency can be improved.
